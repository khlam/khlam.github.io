Bio

Hello and welcome to my personal website! My full name is Kin-Ho Lam, pronounced /kIn/-Hoʊ Lăm. I usually go by my family name, 'Lam'.

I hold a Bachelors of Computer Science (2019) and a Masters of Artificial Intelligence (2022) from Oregon State University. My graduate studies advisor was Professor Alan Fern.

I have extensive experience in software engineering, devops, R&D, and development efforts towards scaling yield on Deep Learning Vision AI. I'm currently focused on the computer vision domain, but I also enjoy tackling search and optimization problems in the RL domain. I am passionate about mentoring and optimizing AI business units to achieve a more efficient 'tooth-to-tail' ratio, enhancing the balance between direct AI-driven outcomes and the business's needs. In my free time I enjoy 3D printing, flying drones, programming, weight lifting, and playing video games.
